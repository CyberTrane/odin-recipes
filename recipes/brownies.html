Brownies

Description

Experience warm, chocolatey goodness with this simple and easy to follow recipe!

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Prep chocolate
  2. Melt chocolate and butter
  3. Add sugar
  4. Add eggs and vanilla
  5. Add flour
  6. Mix the batter
  7. Bake and cool
