Cheesecake

Description

If you like cheesecakes with smooth, rich, and creamy textures, and crisy graham cracker crust, then look no further!

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Sour cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs
  • Graham crackers
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring ingredients to room temperature
  2. Ground graham crackers for crust
  3. Create your cheesecake filling
  4. Bake your cheesecake
  5. Chill your cheesecake
