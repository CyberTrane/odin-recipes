Apple Pie

Description

Apple pie is the classic sweet and warm dessert we all crave!

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Pie crust
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Prepare shell pie crust
  3. Melt butter
  4. Add and mix flour, water, sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla
  5. Add the apples
  6. Fill pie crust with mixture
  7. Cover pie and bake
